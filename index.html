Bioinformatics

I established an developed an interactive and novel transcription factor (TF) regulatory network analysis to help visualize how TF from differentialy expressed genes (DEGs) regulate other DEGs Down regulated genes (Blue); Up regulated genes (red); Size represents Fold change

I coined and pioneered the analysis "COVERT" (Chromosome Observation and Visualization of Enriched RNA Transcripts) where DEGs are clustered based on genomic distance and fold change with clustering algorithm
